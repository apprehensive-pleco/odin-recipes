Southern Fried Chicken

A Delicious Southern Fan Fare!

Salty Breaded Chicken that is Hearty

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable or Canola Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients!
  2. Stir the Eggs
  3. Dip the Chicken in Flour
  4. Dredge in Eggs
  5. Dip in flour again
  6. Fry in the Oil
  7. Enjoy!
