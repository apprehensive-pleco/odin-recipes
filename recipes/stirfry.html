Stir Fry

A Delicious Asian Fan Fare!

A sweet and salty and sour treat that makes its home in alot of Asian Regions with variations that include beef, chicken, shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Chicken, Beef, or Shrimp
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Rice or Noodles
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients!
  2. Stir Fry the Rice
  3. Stir Fry the Veggies
  4. Add the Seasonings
  5. Enjoy!
